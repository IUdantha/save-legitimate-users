PRIVACY NOTICE

Our Pledge of Discretion & Security
To protect the integrity of our auctions and the privacy of our clientele, we have instituted a mandatory verification process. This is the foundation of the high-trust environment www.agam.art provides.

1. The Purpose of Verification
We collect your identification and financial capacity information for two specific and necessary purposes:

  • To Grant Bidding Approval: To verify your identity and ensure you meet the criteria for a vetted buyer.
  • To Comply with NZ Law: To meet our strict legal duties under the Anti-Money Laundering and Countering Financing of Terrorism Act 2009.

This information is used for verification only and is never used for marketing or profiling.

2. Our Data Security Pledge
We consider the protection of your data a non-negotiable obligation.

  • Security: All documents are secured with end-to-end encryption. Our systems are built to industry-leading security standards.
  • Access: Access to this information is strictly limited to our dedicated in-house compliance team and is logged and audited.
  • Retention: Your verification documents are retained only for as long as necessary to comply with New Zealand law, after which they are securely and permanently deleted.

3. Your Rights & Control
In accordance with the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and request the correction of your personal information. To do so, please contact our Privacy Officer directly.

For a comprehensive overview of our data practices, please see our full Privacy Policy.
